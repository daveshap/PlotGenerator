1. Opening Image: The small town of Shelbyville in the heat of summer. Two children, a boy and a girl, are playing together.

2. Theme Stated: The girl, Scout, and the boy, Jem, stumble upon a man sitting on a porch. He introduces himself as Arthur "Boo" Radley. He tells them stories of his life growing up in Africa.

3. Setup: The two children are captivated by his tales. They are excited to have made a new friend. However, their father, Atticus Finch, warns them to stay away from Boo. Atticus is a lawyer, and he is currently defending a black man accused of a crime he did not commit. Because of this, the town is dangerous and filled with prejudice.

4. Catalyst: One night, Jem and Scout are awakened by a noise outside. They peek through the window to see a man walking around their house. It is Boo Radley! Jem is terrified, but Scout is fascinated.

5. Debate: Atticus tells Jem and Scout that they must be brave in the face of adversity. However, the town is still dangerous. Jem and Scout find themselves the targets of mean-spirited pranks and jokes.

6. Break Into Two: One day, as they are walking home from school, they are attacked by a mob of white men. They are saved by the sudden appearance of Boo Radley, who scares the men away.

7. B Story: Jem and Scout are now convinced that Boo is a friend, and they start leaving presents for him in a tree outside his house. One day, they find their presents gone, and in their place is a gift for them: a sewing kit for Scout and a pocket watch for Jem.

8. Fun and Games: The town is still on edge, but Jem and Scout continue to leave presents for Boo Radley. They wait for him to come out of his house.

9. Midpoint: Atticus is attacked by a rabid dog. Boo Radley kills the dog with a knife, saving Atticus's life.

10. Bad Guys Close In: Boo Radley is now a town hero, and he is invited to the Finch house for dinner. Jem and Scout are finally able to talk to him and thank him for saving their father.

11. All is Lost: However, as Boo is leaving, his father becomes angry and violent. He threatens to hurt Jem and Scout.

12. Dark Night of the Soul: Boo Radley intervenes and saves them. He is now a hero in the eyes of Jem and Scout.

13. Break Into Three: The town is still dangerous, but Jem and Scout continue to leave presents for Boo Radley. They wait for him to come out of his house.

14. Finale: One day, Boo Radley comes out of his house and talks to Jem and Scout. He says that he hopes to see them again soon.

15. Final Image: The three of them together, talking and laughing.
