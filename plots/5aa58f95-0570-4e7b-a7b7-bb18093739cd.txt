1. Opening Image: The story begins with the arrival of a strange object on the planet Zoron. The object is a small stone statue of a creature that is unknown to the Zoronians.

2. Theme Stated: The family is very curious about the statue and they begin to ask questions about it. Darrin's father, a famous archaeologist, decides to take the statue to his friend, Dr. Klaren, to see if he can identify it.

3. Setup: Dr. Klaren is able to identify the statue as a creature from a long-lost planet called Earth. The news of the statue spreads quickly and soon the whole planet is talking about it.

4. Catalyst: The Zoronians are very excited about the prospect of meeting creatures from another planet. They send out a message to the Earthlings, inviting them to come and visit their world.

5. Debate: A spaceship from Earth arrives on Zoron and is met by a warm welcome from the Zoronians. The Earthlings are amazed by the planet and its strange inhabitants. They are even more amazed when they learn that the Zoronians have the ability to travel through time.

6. Break Into Two: The Earthlings are given a tour of the planet and are introduced to the different time periods that the Zoronians can visit. They are also shown the statues of the Earth creatures that the Zoronians have found.

7. B Story: The Earthlings are excited to learn about the different time periods and are eager to visit them. Darrin's father takes the Earthlings to the period of the dinosaurs.

8. Fun and Games: The Earthlings are amazed by the sight of the dinosaurs and are excited to learn about them.

9. Midpoint: The Earthlings also visit the period of the Ancient Egyptians. They are impressed by the pyramids and the temples. They also meet the Pharaohs and learn about their culture.

10. Bad Guys Close In: eventually decide to go back to their own time.

11. All is Lost: The Earthlings say goodbye to the Zoronians and board their spaceship. As they leave, Darrin's father gives them the statue of the creature from Earth.

12. Dark Night of the Soul: The Earthlings return to their own time and are welcomed back by their families and friends. They tell them about their amazing adventure on Zoron and about the different time periods that they visited.

13. Break Into Three: The Earthlings are excited to share their experience with others and to learn more about the different time periods.

14. Finale: Darrin's father takes the Earthlings to the period of the dinosaurs.

15. Final Image: The Earthlings are amazed by the sight of the dinosaurs and are excited to learn about them.
