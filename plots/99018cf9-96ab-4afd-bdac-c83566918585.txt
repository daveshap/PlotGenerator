1. Opening Image: The Mediterranean Sea is a hotbed of activity, with pirates, privateers, and explorers all vying for a piece of the action.

2. Theme Stated: The villagers of a small fishing village on the coast of Tunisia are a hardy and independent people.

3. Setup: The village is the perfect target for a young ambitious pirate captain.

4. Catalyst: The pirate captain and his crew descend on the village, plundering and pillaging.

5. Debate: The villagers fight back as best they can, but they are no match for the pirates.

6. Break Into Two: The villagers are left angry and bitter, but they refuse to give up. They rebuild their village and vow to never be taken advantage of again.

7. B Story: As the years pass, the village prospers. It becomes a thriving community, with a thriving fishing industry.

8. Fun and Games: One day, a ship carrying a group of Explorers from a wealthy country crashes on the reef just offshore. The villagers go to their aid, and despite the language barrier, they manage to communicate.

9. Midpoint: The Explorers are grateful for the help, and they reward the villagers handsomely. As a result, the village becomes even more prosperous.

10. Bad Guys Close In: The villagers start to think that maybe they could take advantage of their newfound wealth and influence. But they soon realize that they are in over their heads. The Explorers are not to be trifled with, and they have their own agenda.

11. All is Lost: The villagers are once again taken advantage of, and they are angry and bitter. But they refuse to give up.

12. Dark Night of the Soul: The villagers use their knowledge of the sea to track down the Explorers, and they defeat them in a fierce battle.

13. Break Into Three: The villagers take back what is rightfully theirs, and they vow never to be taken advantage of again.

14. Finale: The villagers continue to prosper, and they develop a reputation for being a fierce and independent people. They are also known for their hospitality and helpfulness.

15. Final Image: The villagers live happily ever after, and they never forget the lessons they had learned.
