1. Opening Image: Sorana is working in the mines, back-breaking labor that she has resigned herself to.

2. Theme Stated: Sorana's master, a sorcerer, tells her that a magical stone she found can grant her wishes.

3. Setup: Sorana is skeptical but decides to make a wish anyway. She wishes to be free from her slavery.

4. Catalyst: Sorana is instantly teleported to a different part of the planet, where she find herself in the middle of a battlefield.

5. Debate: Sorana does not know how she got there, or what is going on, but she knows that she must fight for her freedom.

6. Break Into Two: Sorana quickly dispatches her first opponent, and then another, and another. She is a skilled warrior, and soon the tide of battle turns in her favor.

7. B Story: The other slaves in the mines are also fighting, and they soon defeat their oppressors. They are free!

8. Fun and Games: Sorana is hailed as a hero, and she is given a position of leadership among the slaves. She leads them to a nearby city, where they take over the government and establish a new society.

9. Midpoint: The sorcerers of the other planets in the solar system are angry that their slaves have been freed, and they band together to wage war on Sorana and her people.

10. Bad Guys Close In: Sorana is vastly outnumbered and outmatched, but she is a skilled warrior and a powerful sorceress.

11. All is Lost: The battle is fierce, and many lives are lost, but in the end Sorana and her people are victorious.

12. Dark Night of the Soul: Sorana is hailed as a hero once again, but her peace is shattered when a new enemy arrives from a distant galaxy.

13. Break Into Three: Sorana must once again marshal her forces and lead them into battle against this new foe.

14. Finale: The fight is long and arduous, but in the end Sorana and her people are victorious.

15. Final Image: Sorana is hailed as a hero once again, and she is revered as a great leader and warrior.
