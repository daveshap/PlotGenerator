1. Opening Image: Sasha is sitting in her apartment, looking at pictures of her and her ex-boyfriend. She's feeling sad and nostalgic. 
2. Theme Stated: Sasha's best friend comes over and tries to cheer her up. She tells Sasha that she needs to get out and live her life. 
3. Setup: Sasha's friend suggests that they take a trip to Europe. Sasha is hesitant at first, but she eventually agrees. 
4. Catalyst: While exploring a cathedral in Europe, Sasha comes across a hidden passage that leads to a secret room. 
5. Debate: Sasha debates whether or not she should go into the room. She's curious but also scared. 
6. Break Into Two: Sasha decides to go into the room. 
7. B Story: Inside the room, Sasha finds a hidden cache of ancient documents. She starts to examine the documents and quickly realizes that they are blueprints for a mysterious and sophisticated machine. 
8. Fun and Games: Sasha is excited by her discovery. She starts to piece together the clues and realizes that the machine is a time machine. 
9. Midpoint: Sasha decides that she wants to use the machine to travel back in time and observe the past. 
10. Bad Guys Close In: Sasha sets the machine for the Bronze age and steps inside. She finds herself in a bustling market in a large city. She's surprised by how advanced the civilization is. 
11. All is Lost: Sasha is horrified by what she sees and decides that she needs to do something to help the women of this time period. 
12. Dark Night of the Soul: Sasha finds a group of women who are gathered together, talking and planning. They seem to be planning some sort of rebellion. Sasha approaches them and asks if she can help. 
13. Break Into Three: Sasha sets the machine for the future and goes back to get what they need. Once she has the supplies, she goes back to the Bronze age and helps the women carry out their plan. 
14. Finale: The rebellion is a success and the women are able to achieve equality. Sasha is content knowing that she's made a difference, but she's also homesick. She decides to go back to her own time. 
15. Final Image: Sasha steps out of the time machine and is surprised to see her long-time boyfriend there waiting for her. He explains that he'd been thinking about her a lot and realized that he made a mistake. He wants to get back together. Sasha is happy and relieved.
