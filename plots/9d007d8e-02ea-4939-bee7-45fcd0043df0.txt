1. Opening Image: Johan is living in the 14th century in the country of Brazil. He is fascinated by stories of far-off lands and is determined to see the world for himself.

2. Theme Stated: Johan's parents try to dissuade him from going on an adventure, but they ultimately allow him to go.

3. Setup: Johan sets sail from the port town of Rio de Janeiro.

4. Catalyst: Johan finds himself in the midst of a great storm.

5. Debate: Johan and the other survivors debate whether to stay on the small island they are stranded on or to try to make it back to the mainland.

6. Break Into Two: Johan decides to build a raft and set out to sea.

7. B Story: Johan is rescued by a ship and taken to the port of Lisbon.

8. Fun and Games: Johan spends the next few years in Lisbon, working as a sailor. He longs to go on another adventure, but he does not have the opportunity.

9. Midpoint: Johan hears about a treasure that is hidden in the jungles of Brazil.

10. Bad Guys Close In: Johan begins his journey in Rio de Janeiro, but he quickly realizes that he is out of his depth. The jungle is dangerous and difficult to navigate.

11. All is Lost: Johan is soon lost and nearly dies of starvation.

12. Dark Night of the Soul: Johan is rescued by a tribe of indigenous people. He learns their language and their way of life.

13. Break Into Three: Johan falls in love with a woman named Mara. He decides to stay with the tribe.

14. Finale: Johan decides to make one last attempt to find the treasure. This time, he is successful. He finds a great treasure, but he is also bitten by a poisonous snake.

15. Final Image: Johan knows that he is going to die. He says goodbye to Mara and the tribe, and he dies contentedly, knowing that he has lived a good life.
