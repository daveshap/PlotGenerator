2. Theme Stated: Aurora is interested in the world-building of her city.
3. Setup: Aurora is shy and awkward, and doesn't quite know how to navigate the social scene.
4. Catalyst: Aurora has a chance encounter with a stranger who tells her about a hidden world that exists within the city.
5. Debate: Aurora is intrigued and begins to look for this hidden world.
6. Break Into Two: Aurora finds a secret entrance that leads her into an amazing and dangerous adventure.
7. B Story: Aurora's journey takes her through many different parts of the city, and she has many exciting and dangerous adventures.
8. Fun and Games: Aurora meets a cast of colorful characters, both friends and enemies, who help her to learn more about the hidden world she is exploring.
9. Midpoint: Aurora starts to uncover the secrets of the city. She discovers that the hidden world is actually a parallel universe that exists alongside the city she knows.
10. Bad Guys Close In: Aurora is determined to find a way back to her own world, but as she gets closer to her goal, she realizes that the price of success may be higher than she is willing to pay.
11. All is Lost: Aurora's journey comes to a climax as she finally faces her greatest challenge: finding the courage to return to the everyday world and share her discoveries with the people she loves.
12. Dark Night of the Soul: Although it is difficult, Aurora ultimately decides that it is worth it to share her adventure with the people she cares about.
13. Break Into Three: Aurora tells them about the hidden world she discovered, and the wonders and dangers she encountered along the way.
14. Finale: The people she shares her story with are amazed and inspired by her courage, and they start to explore the city in search of the hidden world themselves.
15. Final Image: Aurora has succeeded in opening up a whole new world of adventure for her loved ones, and her story will continue to be told for generations to come.
