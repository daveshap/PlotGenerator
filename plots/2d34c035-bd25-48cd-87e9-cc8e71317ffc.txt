1. Opening Image: Nebet is introduced as a young priestess in Egypt who is coming of age in a time of great change. The country is in a state of flux, and Nebet is introspective and thoughtful.

2. Theme Stated: Nebet often finds herself questioning the old ways.

3. Setup: Nebet's best friend, Heshwi, is married off to a wealthy nobleman. Nebet is heartbroken.

4. Catalyst: Nebet has a dream in which she is visited by the goddess Isis. Isis tells Nebet that she has a special destiny, and that she must go on a quest to find a lost city that is hidden in the desert.

5. Debate: Nebet is hesitant at first, but she eventually agrees to go on the quest.

6. Break Into Two: Nebet begins her journey, accompanied by her faithful cat, Bast.

7. B Story: As Nebet and Bast travel across the desert, they meet a man named Tahar. Tahar is a historian who has been studying the lost city for many years.

8. Fun and Games: Nebet and Tahar explore the ruins of the city, and they gradually piece together its history.

9. Midpoint: Nebet and Tahar discover that the city was once a thriving metropolis, but it was eventually abandoned due to a curse.

10. Bad Guys Close In: Nebet and Tahar start to develop feelings for each other.

11. All is Lost: Nebet realizes that she must return to her life in Egypt. She says goodbye to Tahar and returns to the temple.

12. Dark Night of the Soul: Nebet has changed after her adventure in the lost city. She is no longer content with just following the old ways.

13. Break Into Three: Nebet starts to question the beliefs of her religion, and she eventually decides to leave the temple.

14. Finale: Nebet and Heshwi meet again, and they reminisce about their childhood together. They both realize that they have changed and that they are no longer the same people they once were.

15. Final Image: Nebet eventually settles down with Tahar, and they live happily together in the lost city of Osiris.
