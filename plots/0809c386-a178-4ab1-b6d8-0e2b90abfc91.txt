2. Theme Stated: Tupaia is solemn and thoughtful in the aftermath of the battle. He knows that this is only one battle in what will be a long and difficult war. As the war wears on, Tupaia becomes more and more battle-hardened and cynical. 3. Setup: Tupaia continues to fight on, leading his people to victory after victory. One day, after a particularly brutal battle, Tupaia is grievously wounded and is left for dead on the battlefield.

4. Catalyst: However, he is rescued by a sympathetic enemy warrior and nursed back to health. This act of kindness changes Tupaia, and he realizes that there is more to this war than meets the eye.

5. Debate: Tupaia starts to investigate the reasons behind the war and, as he does, he uncovers a dark and sinister plot. He realizes that the war is being fought for the benefit of a few powerful men and that it is based on lies and misinformation.

6. Break Into Two: Tupaia rallies his people and leads them in a final, decisive battle against the enemy. This time, they are not fighting for revenge or for blood, but for the freedom and the future of their people.

7. B Story: We see the development of Tupaia's character throughout the story, from brooding cynicism to patriotism and hope.

8. Fun and Games: The action-packed, pulse-pounding story of heroism and patriots set in a time of great conflict is sure to leave readers breathless.

9. Midpoint: Tupaia's people are victorious in the final battle and Tupaia is hailed as a savior and a hero.
