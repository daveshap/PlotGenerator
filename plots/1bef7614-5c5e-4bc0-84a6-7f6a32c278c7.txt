1. Opening Image: We see the Riddler, a mysterious figure, interrogating a man. The man is wounded and looks terrified.

2. Theme Stated: The Riddler wants to know the location of a treasure. He believes that the man knows where it is hidden and is determined to find it.

3. Setup: We are introduced to the Riddler's backstory. He is a bandit who has been terrorizing villages in India. He asks his riddle and kills those who can't answer it.

4. Catalyst: The Riddler comes to a small village and asks his riddle. But this time, a young boy is able to answer it.

5. Debate: The Riddler is shocked that someone has finally been able to solve his riddle. He is even more shocked when the boy tells him that he had heard the answer from a god.

6. Break Into Two: The Riddler doesn't believe in gods, but he is intrigued by the boy's story. He asks him where he had heard the answer from, and the boy tells him that he had dreamt it.

7. B Story: The Riddler is fascinated by the boy's story and asks him to tell him more about his dream. The boy tells him that he had dreamt that he was in a land of eternal fire, and that he had heard a voice telling him the answer to the riddler's question.

8. Fun and Games: The Riddler is amazed by the boy's story and asks him to lead him to this land of eternal fire. The boy takes him to a nearby mountain, and the Riddler sees that there is a fire burning at the top of it.

9. Midpoint: The Riddler is convinced that this is the place where the boy had heard the voice, and he is determined to find out more. He climbs to the top of the mountain and enters the fire.

10. Bad Guys Close In: The Riddler is immediately incinerated, but as he dies, he hears the voice again. The voice tells him that the answer to his riddle is "life."

11. All is Lost: The Riddler realizes that he has been searching for the wrong thing all along, and that the true treasure is not a material thing, but the knowledge that he had gained.

12. Dark Night of the Soul: The Riddler is confronted with the truth of his life and realizes that he has been wasting his time.

13. Break Into Three: The Riddler decides to change his ways and dedicate his life to finding the true treasure.

14. Finale: The Riddler sets out on a new quest, determined to find the true treasure.

15. Final Image: The Riddler is seen searching for the true treasure, his eyes full of determination.
