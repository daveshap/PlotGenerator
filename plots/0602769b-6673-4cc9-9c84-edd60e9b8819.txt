1. Opening Image: A group of astronauts are on a space station. They are exploring an abandoned station when they find markers from an ancient culture.

2. Theme Stated: The astronauts debate what the markers mean. Is it a message? A warning? A call to arms?

3. Setup: The debate rages on until a second, more shocking discovery is made. Hidden in the shadows of one of Saturn's moons is a derelict space station, abandoned and in ruins.

4. Catalyst: A team of astronauts is dispatched to investigate the space station. They quickly realize that something is very wrong. The station is not abandoned, it's inhabited by a group of cultists who believe that the end of the world is coming.

5. Debate: The team is divided on what to do, some members want to confront the cultists and try to reason with them, while others want to get away as quickly as possible.

6. Break Into Two: The situation quickly deteriorates when the cultists take one of the astronauts hostage and threaten to kill her if the team doesn't do as they're told.

7. B Story: The team is forced to give in to the cultists demands and they start making plans to leave Earth. But before they can leave, they need to find a way to subdue the cultists and take back control of the space station.

8. Fun and Games: The team comes up with a plan to retake the space station. They execute the plan, but it goes wrong and they find themselves in a desperate battle for their lives against the cultists.

9. Midpoint: The team is outnumbered and outgunned. They must use all their skills and training to survive.

10. Bad Guys Close In: The cultists are closing in on the team. They are armed and dangerous, and they're not afraid to use violence to get what they want.

11. All is Lost: The team is forced to retreat. They are traumatized by what they've seen and faced.

12. Dark Night of the Soul: The team is divided on what to do next. Some members want to confront the cultists and try to reason with them, while others want to get away as quickly as possible.

13. Break Into Three: The team comes up with a plan to retake the space station. They execute the plan and are successful in taking back the space station.

14. Finale: The team makes it back to Earth. They are the only ones left to tell the world about the cultists and their warning of the coming apocalypse.

15. Final Image: The team is standing on the Earth, looking up at the space station.
