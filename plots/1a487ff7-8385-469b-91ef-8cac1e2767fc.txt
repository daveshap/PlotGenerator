1. Opening Image: Nicolas is in his father's hospital, watching him save a patient's life. He is inspired by his father's work and decides that he wants to become a doctor.

2. Theme Stated: Nicolas's father tells him that being a doctor is not just about saving lives, but about helping people in their darkest hour.

3. Setup: Nicolas begins his studies at a medical school in Brazil. He quickly realizes that he is not cut out for the academic life and struggles to keep up with his classmates.

4. Catalyst: The local cartel threatens Nicolas's family, forcing him to drop out of school and join their ranks.

5. Debate: Nicolas is torn between his sense of duty to his family and his desire to be a doctor. He eventually decides to join the cartel in order to protect his family.

6. Break Into Two: Nicolas becomes a cartel enforcer. He quickly realizes that the life of violence is not for him, but he is trapped by his sense of duty.

7. B Story: Nicolas meets and falls in love with a woman named Maria. She is a resistance fighter who is fighting against the cartel's tyrannical rule.

8. Fun and Games: Nicolas begins to help the resistance fighters, using his medical skills to help the injured and using his inside knowledge of the cartel to help the resistance take them down.

9. Midpoint: The resistance's fight against the cartel escalates, and Nicolas finds himself in the middle of the bloodshed. He quickly realizes that the life of a resistance fighter is not for him, but he is trapped by his sense of duty.

10. Bad Guys Close In: The cartel's leader orders a massacre of innocent civilians. Nicolas has had enough and betrays the resistance, rejoining the cartel in order to stop the leader.

11. All is Lost: The leader is enraged by Nicolas's betrayal and vows to kill him. The two engage in a deadly duel, but in the end, the leader is victorious. Nicolas is left seriously injured and is once again left for dead.

12. Dark Night of the Soul: Nicolas is found and rescued by the resistance fighters he once betrayed. They take him in and nurse him back to health. Nicolas is deeply remorseful for his actions and wants to make things right.

13. Break Into Three: Nicolas joins the resistance once again and helps them in their fight against the leader. However, the leader is a formidable opponent and the resistance is outnumbered and outgunned.

14. Finale: In the end, the resistance is defeated and the leader is killed. Nicolas is the only survivor. He is left traumatized by the violence and bloodshed, but he is also free.

15. Final Image: Nicolas is back in his father's hospital, watching him save a patient's life. He is reminded of his father's words and knows that he wants to become a doctor.
