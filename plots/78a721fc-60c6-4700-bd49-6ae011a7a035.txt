1. Opening Image: The year is 2155 and the world is a vastly different place than it is today. For starters, humans have now colonized the solar system and beyond. In fact, the first human settlement on Mars was established over a century ago and life on the red planet is thriving.

2. Theme Stated: The United Earth government governs all human settlements in the solar system and maintains peace and order through a highly advanced artificial intelligence program known as the AI constables. But there is one settlement that is not under the jurisdiction of the United Earth government and that is the orbital station known as Mondo.

3. Setup: Mondo is a haven for criminals, outcasts, and miscreants of all kinds. It is a lawless place where anything goes and the strong prey on the weak. The only rule on Mondo is that there are no rules.

4. Catalyst: When a talented young woman named Akoni is forced to flee her home on Earth after a tragic incident, she ends up on Mondo.

5. Debate: Akoni is not sure if she wants to stay on Mondo or try to find a way back to Earth. She is debating whether to give up on her dream of being a pilot or to take a chance and stay on Mondo.

6. Break Into Two: Akoni decides to stay on Mondo and quickly proves herself to be a talented pilot and a valuable member of Jiro's team.

7. B Story: Akoni's sister is kidnapped by a rival smuggling gang led by the ruthless Kano.

8. Fun and Games: Akoni mounts a daring rescue mission that quickly escalates into an all-out war between the two gangs.

9. Midpoint: With the help of Jiro and his team, Akoni not only rescues her sister, but takes down Kano and his gang once and for all.

10. Bad Guys Close In: Akoni is now a respected leader in her own right and she and Jiro continue to run their successful smuggling operation on Mondo. They have become a force to be reckoned with and no one dares to cross them.

11. All is Lost: But there are always new challenges to face and new enemies to fight.

12. Dark Night of the Soul: Akoni is faced with a new challenge when a rival gang leader kidnaps her sister.
