1. Opening Image: Clara is born into a wealthy family in the early 1800s in Italy. She is doted on by her parents and grows up enjoying all the privileges that come with her social status.

2. Theme Stated: Despite her comfortable life, Clara has a restless spirit and a yearning for adventure.

3. Setup: Clara is presented to society at the age of 18 and becomes a debutante. She attends lavish balls and parties, but none of the suitors catch her eye.

4. Catalyst: At the age of 23, Clara meets a young man named Roberto at a party and they instantly hit it off.

5. Debate: Clara is torn between her feelings for Roberto and her desire for adventure.

6. Break Into Two: Clara decides to marry Roberto and they become engaged.

7. B Story: Clara's parents are thrilled that she has finally found a suitable match.

8. Fun and Games: The wedding plans are underway when Roberto suddenly breaks off the engagement, leaving Clara heartbroken.

9. Midpoint: Clara's parents receive a letter from Roberto explaining that he had to break off the engagement because he is actually in love with another woman.

10. Bad Guys Close In: Clara is hurt and angry, but she eventually recovers from the experience and comes out of her period of mourning.

11. All is Lost: A few years later, when she is 27, Clara meets a man named Angelo at a party. Angelo is a handsome, well-educated, and wealthy. He is also a bit of a playboy and is known for his numerous affairs.

12. Dark Night of the Soul: Clara is torn between her feelings for Angelo and her desire for a traditional family.

13. Break Into Three: Clara decides to break off the relationship with Angelo and she leaves Italy to have their baby in secret.

14. Finale: Clara gives birth to a baby girl and she is absolutely thrilled. She names her daughter Severine and raises her in a small village in Italy.

15. Final Image: As Severine grows up, she is spoiled by her doting mother and grandparents. She is a beautiful and talented girl, but she has a wild streak.
