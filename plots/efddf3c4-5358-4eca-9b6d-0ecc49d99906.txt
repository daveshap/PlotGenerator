1. Opening Image: We see the city of Brazil, full of life and color. We then see our main character, Maria, playing with her friends. They are laughing and having a good time.

2. Theme Stated: The story is about family, love, and loss.

3. Setup: We are introduced to Maria and her friends. We learn about their lives and what they like to do.

4. Catalyst: The girls hear a noise and follow it to find a baby in a basket. The baby is crying and alone.

5. Debate: Maria and her friends debate what to do with the baby. They eventually decide to take her in and care for her.

6. Break Into Two: Maria and her friends take the baby back to her house and start to care for her. They name her Ana.

7. B Story: We learn about Ana's life as she grows up. She is happy and loved, but she is always curious about her real parents.

8. Fun and Games: Ana is a teenager and she finds a diary hidden in her mother's jewelry box. The diary is in Portuguese, but Ana is able to decipher some of it.

9. Midpoint: Ana learns from the diary that her mother was a poor girl from the countryside who was sold into marriage. Her husband was a cruel man who beat her. Ana is horrified by this story.

10. Bad Guys Close In: Ana is determined to find out more about her mother and her family. She travels to the rural town where her mother was from and starts asking questions.

11. All is Lost: Ana's half-brother is living in a remote village with a family who is barely scraping by.

12. Dark Night of the Soul: Ana is sad and frustrated. She doesn't know how to help her half-brother.

13. Break Into Three: Ana decides to write her mother's story. She hopes that by sharing her mother's story, she can help others who have been through similar experiences.

14. Finale: Ana finishes writing her mother's story. She shares it with her half-brother and they are finally able to connect.

15. Final Image: Ana and her half-brother are living together in the city. They are happy and have a bright future ahead of them.
