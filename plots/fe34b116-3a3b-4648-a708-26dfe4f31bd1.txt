1. Opening Image: We see the kingdom in all its glory, with the wise and just king ruling over it. All is well in the land, until a dark force begins to stir.

2. Theme Stated: The theme of the story is the battle between good and evil.

3. Setup: We are introduced to the main characters and the conflict that they will face. The evil wizard has taken control of the kingdom and the people are living in fear.

4. Catalyst: The king sets out on a journey to find a hero who can help him defeat the wizard.

5. Debate: The heroes debate whether or not they should help the king.

6. Break Into Two: The heroes agree to help the king and they set out to defeat the evil wizard.

7. B Story: The group of brave warriors sets out to find the heroes and help them defeat the wizard.

8. Fun and Games: The heroes and their allies fight bravely against the wizard and his forces.

9. Midpoint: The heroes are able to drive the wizard back, but he soon returns with an even larger army.

10. Bad Guys Close In: The heroes are not able to defeat the wizard and he takes control of the kingdom.

11. All is Lost: The king and his daughter are captured and the wizard imposes his will on the people.

12. Dark Night of the Soul: The heroes continue to fight the wizard and they are eventually able to rescue the king and his daughter.

13. Break Into Three: With the help of the brave warriors, the heroes are able to defeat the wizard and drive him out of the kingdom.

14. Finale: The kingdom is saved, but at great cost. The heroes have paid a heavy price, but the people are free.

15. Final Image: The people of the kingdom are living happily ever after, and they are careful to guard against the wizard's return.
