1. Opening Image: The sun is rising slowly over the horizon on the surface of the moon, casting a long shadow over the craters and plains. The earth is a blue-green dot in the distance, barely visible through the haze.

2. Theme Stated: Lunaris, Lord of the Moon, has come to claim what is rightfully his.

3. Setup: Lunaris is a strange and otherworldly man with bright green eyes and long, silver hair. He wears a flowing robe of blue and silver, embroidered with stars.

4. Catalyst: Lunaris begins to gather an army of moon creatures, called lunatics.

5. Debate: Lunaris knows that the earth's armies will be no match for his own. But as Lunaris's plans begin to come together, he begins to realize that there is one person who could stop him: the mysterious Moon Maiden.

6. Break Into Two: Lunaris sets out to find the Moon Maiden.

7. B Story: The Moon Maiden is a legend among the lunatics. She is said to be a powerful witch who lives on the dark side of the moon.

8. Fun and Games: Lunaris's search for the Moon Maiden leads him to the dark side of the moon, where she is said to live. There, in a hidden cave, he finds her. The Moon Maiden is younger than he expected, and she is very beautiful.

9. Midpoint: Lunaris tries to convince the Moon Maiden to join his side, but she refuses. She tells him that his plans are evil and that she would never help him.

10. Bad Guys Close In: Enraged, Lunaris attacks the Moon Maiden. But she is too powerful for him. She uses her magic to bind him in chains and cast him out of her cave.

11. All is Lost: Lunaris is defeated, but he vows that he would one day return. The Moon Maiden knows that she has won this battle, but the war is far from over.

12. Dark Night of the Soul: Lunaris is despondent after his defeat. He wanders the surface of the moon, lost and alone.

13. Break Into Three: Lunaris's despair is interrupted by the arrival of his army of lunatics. They have come to help him in his quest to conquer the earth.

14. Finale: Lunaris and his army of lunatics launch their assault on the earth. But the earth's armies are prepared for them. The Moon Maiden uses her magic to turn the tide of the battle. Lunaris is defeated once again and his army is scattered.

15. Final Image: The Moon Maiden stands victorious over the defeated Lunaris. The earth is safe, for now. But the war is far from over.
