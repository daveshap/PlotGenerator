1. Opening Image: We see the small village of Mango, Brazil. It is a peaceful place surrounded by jungle.

2. Theme Stated: We see Isabella, a mischievous child who loves to play pranks and explore her surroundings.

3. Setup: One day, while climbing a tree, Isabella spots a strange object in the distance. She climbs down and runs to tell her friends, who all agree to follow her to see what she has found.

4. Catalyst: As they approach the object, they see that it is a large iron door set into the side of a hill. They try to open it, but it is locked.

5. Debate: Isabella's friends want to go back to the village and tell the adults, but Isabella is determined to find a way inside.

6. Break Into Two: Isabella spends the next few days trying to find a key to the door, but she is unsuccessful. Finally, she decides to ask her father if he knows anything about the door.

7. B Story: Her father tells her the story of the temple and the snake god. Isabella begins to realize that the temple is a sacred place.

8. Fun and Games: Despite her father's warnings, Isabella continues to try to find a way into the temple. One day, she follows a snake into a dark passage.

9. Midpoint: Isabella comes to a large room with a statue of a snake god. She is mesmerized by the statue and doesn't hear someone coming up behind her.

10. Bad Guys Close In: Isabella is startled when she feels a hand on her shoulder and she screams. It is her father. He has been looking for her and he is relieved to find her safe.

11. All is Lost: Her father tells her that she is not allowed to come back to the temple. Isabella is sad but decides to respect her father's wishes.

12. Dark Night of the Soul: A few weeks later, there is a terrible earthquake and the temple is destroyed.

13. Break Into Three: Isabella is sad, but she knows that the temple was a sacred place and it is better to leave it in its original state.

14. Finale: Isabella learns a valuable lesson about respect and responsibility.

15. Final Image: We see the ruins of the temple, surrounded by the jungle.
