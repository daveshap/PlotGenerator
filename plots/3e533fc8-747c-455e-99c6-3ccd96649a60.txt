1. Opening Image: We see the magical land of Brazil, full of color and life. We meet our protagonist, Lily, as she is just discovering her own magical powers.

2. Theme Stated: We learn that Lily is a very sweet and kind person, who is always helping others and making them feel comfortable. However, she is also extremely curious and loves to ask questions.

3. Setup: We are introduced to Ricardo, a handsome and charismatic man who is interested in Lily and her powers. Ricardo begins to teach Lily about magic.

4. Catalyst: Lily's powers begin to grow, and she starts to become more confident in her abilities. Ricardo introduces Lily to other magical creatures, such as fairies and trolls.

5. Debate: Lily is torn between her desire to learn more about magic and the dangers that come with it. She eventually decides to go with the good forces and starts her training.

6. Break Into Two: Lily's training with the good forces begins.

7. B Story: Lily's friends from her adventures, Sofia and Fernando, remain by her side.

8. Fun and Games: Lily continues to train and become more powerful. She also starts to discover more about her own magical powers.

9. Midpoint: The evil forces start to become more interested in Lily because of her increasing powers. They begin to send spies to try to find out more about her.

10. Bad Guys Close In: The evil forces attack Lily's village. They kill many people and destroy many homes. Lily is devastated by the attack, but she is determined to stop the evil forces.

11. All is Lost: Lily faces off against the leader of the evil forces. She is outnumbered and outmatched, but she uses her magic to defeat her enemies.

12. Dark Night of the Soul: Lily is injured in the battle and is close to defeat. She is saved by her friends and defeats the evil forces.

13. Break Into Three: Lily decides to stay with the good forces and continue her training.

14. Finale: Lily eventually becomes a great leader and helps people with her powers. She remains friends with her friends from her adventures, and they all live happily ever after.

15. Final Image: We see Lily and her friends living happily ever after in the magical land of Brazil.
