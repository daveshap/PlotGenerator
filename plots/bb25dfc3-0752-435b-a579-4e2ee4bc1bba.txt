1. Opening Image: We see the small town of Puducherry, India. It is a bustling town with people from all walks of life.

2. Theme Stated: The theme of the story is that no matter where you come from, you can always find a place to call home.

3. Setup: We are introduced to Maya, a young woman who is struggling to make ends meet. She takes care of her sick mother and works odd jobs to support them both.

4. Catalyst: One night, she meets a woman named Lakshmi who tells her about a village that is looking for a teacher. Lakshmi says that the village, called Kottakuppam, is a place where people from all over the world come to learn about India's rich culture and history.

5. Debate: Maya is intrigued and decides to apply for the job. When she arrives in Kottakuppam, she is amazed by the diversity of the people there. She meets people from all walks of life, including a woman named Sita who teaches her about the traditional Hindu religion. She also meets a man named Ravi, who is a professor at the local university. Ravi tells her about the conflict between the British and the French colonial powers in India. Maya is fascinated by the history of the country and the people she meets.

6. Break Into Two: As she gets to know the people of Kottakuppam, she begins to realize that there is a dark secret lurking beneath the surface. She learns that the village is built on top of an ancient temple that is believed to be cursed. The temple is said to be home to a demon that would bring death and destruction to anyone who entered it.

7. B Story: Maya is skeptical of the story at first, but she soon realizes that there is something sinister going on in the village. One night, she is walking home from work when she sees a group of men carrying a body into the temple. She is about to go for help when she hears someone calling her name. It is Ravi. He tells her that she needs to leave the village immediately. He says that the men she saw were part of a cult that was planning to sacrifice her to the demon in the temple.

8. Fun and Games: Ravi manages to get Maya out of the village, but the cult members are hot on their heels. They are eventually caught and brought back to Kottakuppam. Maya is horrified to learn that the cult members are planning to sacrifice her mother to the demon in the temple.

9. Midpoint: Maya is determined to save her mother and stop the cult from carrying out their evil plans. She enlists the help of her friends Sita and Ravi. Together, they hatch a plan to infiltrates the temple and rescue Maya's mother.

10. Bad Guys Close In: The plan is risky, but it works. Maya and her friends are able to rescue her mother and stop the cult from sacrificing her to the demon. However, the leader of the cult, a man named Shankar, is not happy. He vows to get revenge on Maya and her friends.

11. All is Lost: Shankar begins to spread rumors about Maya and her friends, saying that they are evil sorcerers who have released the demon from the temple. He also begins to spread rumors about the village, saying that it is cursed and that anyone who stays there will be cursed as well. As the rumors spread, more and more people begin to leave the village.

12. Dark Night of the Soul: Maya and her friends are determined to stop Shankar from destroying the village. They come up with a plan to lure him into the temple and trap him there. Maya pretends to be possessed by the demon and lured Shankar into the temple. Once he was inside, they locked the doors and set the temple on fire. Shankar was killed in the fire and the village was safe. However, the damage was done. The rumors about the village had spread and it was now deserted.

13. Break Into Three: Maya and her friends were the only ones left. Maya and her friends decided to stay in the village and rebuild it. They worked hard to restore the village to its former glory. Slowly but surely, people began to come back to the village. The village was once again thriving.

14. Finale: However, the demon that was trapped in the temple was not happy. It was angry and frustrated at being trapped. It wanted to be free. One night, Maya was awoken by a noise coming from the temple. She went to investigate and saw the demon trying to break out of the temple. The demon saw Maya and began to chase her. Maya ran for her life and managed to escape. She went to warn her friends and they decided to leave the village. They knew that the demon would never stop chasing them and they didn't want to risk the safety of the village.

15. Final Image: As they were leaving, Maya looked back at the village. She knew that she would never forget the village or the people she met there.
